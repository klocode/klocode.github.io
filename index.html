About Me

I am an avid sports fan, musician, dog dad, knowledge seeker, critical thinker, and a problem solver.

After graduating from Purdue University with degrees in Actuarial Science and Applied Statistics, I worked as an auditor in the healthcare industry. While working as an auditor, I found that my interests started to shift. I've always enjoyed building things and understanding how they work inside and out which is what led me to start learning how to code. After taking some computer science courses during my undergraduate studies and then taking more classes later at IUPUI (Indiana University Purdue University Indianapolis) while I worked, I stumbled upon The Iron Yard which offered a 12-week immersive code camp.

Going through The Iron Yard has been one of the most valuable experiences. Through their back end engineering course, I've met and worked with diverse and wonderful individuals and crafted a foundation to pursue a career I am passionate about. I'm excited to be able to continue to learn and can't wait to use my skills to contribute in the workplace.
